# Legal Documents Summary

## Overview
Comprehensive, world-class Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy documents have been created to protect RetailCloud and its operations. These documents are professionally written, legally sound, and provide strong protections against liability.

## Documents Created

### 1. Terms and Conditions
**Location:** 
- Backend template: `backend/templates/legal/terms_and_conditions.html`
- Frontend page: `frontend/src/pages/Legal/TermsAndConditions.tsx`
- Route: `/terms`

**Key Protections:**

#### Liability Limitations
- **Comprehensive Limitation of Liability:** Limits liability to the amount paid in the past 12 months (or $100 minimum)
- **Exclusion of Consequential Damages:** Explicitly excludes indirect, incidental, special, consequential, and punitive damages
- **"As Is" Warranty Disclaimer:** Service provided without warranties of any kind

#### Indemnification
- **Broad Indemnification Clause:** Users indemnify RetailCloud against all claims, damages, losses, and expenses
- **Survival Clause:** Indemnification obligations survive termination

#### User Responsibilities
- Account security and credential protection
- Compliance with acceptable use policy
- Data backup responsibilities
- Compliance with all applicable laws

#### Payment and Subscription Terms
- Clear billing and payment terms
- Non-refundable fees (with exceptions)
- Failed payment consequences
- Price change procedures

#### Termination Rights
- Clear termination procedures for both parties
- Immediate termination for violations
- Data retention and deletion policies

#### Legal Protections
- Force majeure clause
- Severability clause
- Governing law and dispute resolution
- Entire agreement clause
- No waiver clause
- Assignment restrictions
