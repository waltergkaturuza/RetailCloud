## Overview
A complete, automated tax management system that handles all tax calculations, compliance, and reporting for Zimbabwe businesses, integrated with trading profits/losses and expenses.

## Features to Implement

### 1. Tax Configuration
- VAT rates (standard, zero-rated, exempt)
- Income tax rates and brackets
- PAYE rates and thresholds
- Other Zimbabwe-specific taxes (AIDS Levy, NSSA, ZIMDEF)
- Tax registration numbers
- Tax periods and filing frequencies

### 2. Automated Tax Calculations
- **Sales Tax (VAT)**: Automatic calculation on sales
- **Purchase Tax (VAT Input)**: Track VAT on purchases
- **VAT Returns**: Calculate VAT payable/refundable
- **Income Tax**: Calculate based on trading profits
- **PAYE**: Employee tax calculations
- **Other Taxes**: Automated calculation based on transactions

### 3. Tax Liability Tracking
- Accrual-based tax liability tracking
- Automatic accruals from sales/purchases
- Tax due date tracking
- Overdue tax alerts
- Payment tracking

### 4. ZIMRA Compliance
- ZIMRA-compliant reports
- VAT return format
- Income tax calculations
- Tax period management
- Filing reminders

### 5. Integration with P&L
- Automatic expense categorization for tax
- Trading profit/loss calculations
- Taxable income calculation
- Deductible expenses
- Non-deductible expenses tracking

### 6. Tax Calendar
- Tax due dates
- Filing deadlines
- Payment reminders
- Compliance calendar
